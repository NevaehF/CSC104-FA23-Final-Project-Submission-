Repeat To Yourself:

- I focus on progress, not perfection.

- I’m the best parent for my child, which is why they are mine.

- My children don’t want perfection; they want me to be there.

- Some days are better than others; that doesn’t mean I’m a bad parent.

- Tomorrow is a new day.

- My mental and physical health matters as much as my child’s.

- Being frustrated doesn’t make me a bad parent; it makes me human.

- I deserve happiness.

- I’m doing the best I can.


{Feel free to make these into a Vision board or note them down!!!}
